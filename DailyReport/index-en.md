# Privacy Policy

This document defines the handling of personal information for DailyReport (a Chrome extension for work time input support systems).

This extension operates as a browser-based application and may collect and provide personally identifiable information for the purpose of improving system quality and delivering information to users within the tool.

Accordingly, since this extension includes functions that involve the collection of personal information, this Privacy Policy is established and published in accordance with Google's policies.

## Basic Policy

We recognize the importance of personal information and consider the protection of such information to be a social responsibility.  
We comply with applicable laws and regulations regarding personal information and appropriately acquire, use, and manage personal information handled by the application.

## Scope of Application

This Privacy Policy applies to the DailyReport Chrome Extension (hereinafter referred to as "the Software") as of **February 17, 2026**.

## Consent to the Handling of Personal Information

Before installing the application developed by us, users are requested to read this Privacy Policy carefully and use the application only if they agree to its terms.

By installing the application, users are deemed to have agreed to this Privacy Policy.

## Purpose of Use of Personal Information

### Personalization

The Software provides content optimized for each individual user.  
Personal information is collected only to the extent necessary to provide such functionality.

### Improvement of Usability

For the purpose of optimizing the user experience, information related to usage analysis may be collected.  
This may include individual operation history and data entered by the user.

### User Support

In order to respond to user inquiries, operation history and entered data may be accessed and used as necessary.

## Management of Personal Information

### Accuracy of Information

The collected information includes personally identifiable information provided by users through input and authentication processes, and such information is interpreted on the premise that it is accurate.

If incorrect information is directly entered by the user, personalization may be performed based on such information.

### Security Management

The Software stores only the information necessary for its operation on a dedicated internal company server.

This server is maintained and operated so that only personnel involved in development and support can access it.

The security of information provided by other servers or services is ensured by the respective service providers.

## Provision of Personal Information to Third Parties

Personal information collected by the Software is shared only among personnel involved in its development and support.

If an appropriate request is made in accordance with the operational rules of the service provider, information held by the Software or content generated through its operation may be provided to the user, the business entity to which the application is provided, or network administrators.

## Contact Information

For inquiries regarding the Software, please contact us through the official internal support site.
